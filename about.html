The University's Commitment to Accessibility

The University of Toronto provides accommodation for students with documented disabilities as established by the Ontario Human Rights Code (OHRC), the Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act (AODA), and the University of Toronto's Statement of Commitment to Persons with Disabilities.

All students who write quizzes, tests, and exams with accommodations through Accommodated Testing Services have documented disabilities and they must be currently registered with Accessibility Services.

Academic Integrity

Accommodated Testing Services' exam proctors receive extensive, intensive training that focuses on balancing academic accommodation with academic integrity.
