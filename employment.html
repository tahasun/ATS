Employment Opportunities

Application Submission Deadline: Friday, July 19, 2019 at 4:00 PM

Position Overview

Incumbents will supervise the writing of tests and examinations written by students with documented disabilities who are registered with Accessibility Services at the University of Toronto, St. George Campus.

Based on pre-defined training objectives, during the training period, incumbents will work with experienced invigilators and be required to successfully complete the required number of training shifts within the specified timeline.

Applicant Requirments:

Successful applicants must be eligible for CUPE 3902 Unit 1 work and are expected to maintain their eligibility through the duration of the appointment.

To Apply:

Applications may be submitted IN PERSON or BY MAIL and must include:

  • Cover letter
  • Resume
  • ROSI course list/registration status
  • Supplemental Application Form

For More Information and a Detailed Position Summary: Visit the University of Toronto's Career Centre website.

Application Submission Deadline: Wednesday, August 31, 2016 at 5:00 PM
Position Term (Approximate): September 2016 through August 2017

Position Overview

Accommodated Testing Services employs a dedicated team of scribes/readers who are committed to the principles of equity in post-secondary education. Scribes/readers work one-on-one with students with disabilities in a test/exam setting accurately recording the student's dictated test/exam responses and/or accurately reading aloud test/exam material to the student. Respect for academic integrity, ability to collaborate effectively, and superior interpersonal skills are a must.

Applicant Requirments:

Current status as a student at the University of Toronto an asset

To Apply:

Applications may be submitted IN PERSON or BY MAIL and must include:

  • Cover letter
  • Resume
  • ROSI course list/registration status
  • Supplemental Application Form

For More Information and a Detailed Position Summary: Visit the University of Toronto's Career Centre website.
